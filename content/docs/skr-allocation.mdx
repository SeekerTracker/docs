# SKR Allocation Checker

Check your SKR token allocation by .skr domain or wallet address.

## What is SKR?

SKR is the native token of the Solana Seeker ecosystem. Allocations are distributed to SeekerID holders based on various factors.

## How to Check Your Allocation

1. Go to [seekertracker.com/skr](https://seekertracker.com/skr)
2. Enter your .skr domain or wallet address
3. View your allocation details

## Season 1 Stats

The SKR page displays:
- Total staked SKR
- Vault balances
- Your personal allocation
- Staking statistics

## Eligibility

To be eligible for SKR allocation:
- Own a .skr SeekerID
- Complete any required staking
- Meet minimum holding requirements
