# Tokenomics

Understanding how $TRACKER revenue is distributed.

## Revenue Model

The Seeker Tracker protocol collects **1% of all $TRACKER token trading volume** as revenue. This fee is automatically collected on every trade.

## Allocation Details

### Treasury (70%)

Funds allocated for:
- Protocol development
- Operational costs
- Marketing initiatives
- Partnership development
- Future growth

### Staking Rewards (10%)

Distributed to users who stake their $TRACKER tokens:
- Incentivizes long-term holding
- Rewards protocol participation
- Regular distribution schedule

### Snake Rewards (10%)

Prize pool for the Snake game:
- Top players compete for rewards
- Must hold $TRACKER to participate
- Leaderboard-based distribution

### Sweepstakes (10%)

Hourly sweepstakes rewards:
- Eligible range: **1M - 20M $TRACKER**
- Automatic entry for holders
- Random selection each hour
